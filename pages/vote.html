godding / vote

vote · recorded · changeable

vote on what you read.
every vote is recorded.

voting is free, anonymous, and recorded. you can change your vote at any time — and the change is recorded too. every flip, every removal, every retry shows up in your trail with a UTC timestamp. nothing is hidden. your votes, badges, reputation, and the full edit history all live in your browser's local storage — clearable any time. (the federated version, where the trail becomes signed commits in the github repo, is on the build roadmap.)

recording rules · the contract:
  • every vote you cast is logged with a UTC timestamp the second you click.
  • you can flip an upvote to a downvote, or remove either — the change is its own entry.
  • fast flips (vote, then unvote, within 10 seconds) cost reputation. signal, not noise.
  • nothing leaves your device until you choose to push it. nothing is silently overwritten.

your activity, dated

every flip recorded with a UTC timestamp — including ones you later took back. this is the public-style record: append-only, never edited in place. clear it if you want; nothing else can.

how reputation works (honestly)

your reputation is computed locally from real activity. the formula:

  • +2 per vote you cast (up or down — both are signal).
  • +1 per distinct page you visit.
  • +5 per topic you engage with (crime / belief / pages — engaging across topics rewards diversity, not stacking).
  • +3 per distinct day you've returned to the site.
  • −1 per "rapid flip" (vote then unvote within 10 seconds — discourages noise).

badges unlock on simple thresholds. nothing is hidden — open the browser console and run JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('godding.profile')) to see exactly what's stored.

the limitation is honest too: this is per-device. if you vote on your phone and your laptop, they don't know about each other. the build page describes the planned federation — votes as small commits in the swarm repo, with the swarm aggregating them. that turns localStorage into a public trail.
