godding / health

health · the short list

what every doctor agrees on, that almost nobody does.

no superfoods, no hacks, no supplements. the small list of things that, polled across every specialty, would come back nearly unanimous.

the consensus list

  1. sleep 7–9 hours, dark and cool, on a regular schedule. the biggest free intervention available. the most consistently underdone, because it costs opportunity, not money.
  2. move every day. walking counts. lifting things 2–3 times a week counts more.
  3. eat mostly plants, mostly unprocessed, stop before stuffed. the disagreements are at the margins; the centre isn't in dispute.
  4. see other humans, in person, regularly. social isolation has a mortality effect on the order of smoking.
  5. get sun, but not stupidly. 10–20 min outdoor light early in the day. circadian inputs are not optional.
  6. drink less alcohol. any alcohol is a small net loss; large amounts, a large net loss.
  7. don't smoke. don't vape.
  8. handle chronic stress, somehow. meditation, therapy, hobby, dog. modality matters less than persistence.
  9. show up to the boring appointments. they catch cheap problems before they become expensive.
