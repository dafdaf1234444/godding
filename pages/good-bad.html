play · the topology of cooperation

good vs. bad — watch the math.

a small live world on a grid. green cells (cooperators) share what they produce with their neighbours and heal the environment. red cells (defectors) take from neighbours, fight each other, and damage the environment. the parameters decide who wins.

what's happening

  • cooperators get a clustering bonus — more good neighbours → more energy. they share with each other and heal the environment around them.
  • defectors steal from neighbours. but defector neighbours are worse than cooperator neighbours: they fight each other and damage env.
  • empties regenerate slowly. cells with high energy replicate, cells with low energy die.
  • topology: cooperator clusters reinforce. defector clusters self-destruct. boundaries are where the action is.

academic basis: spatial prisoner's dilemma (evolutionary game theory). same family as ants on bodies and swarm.
