justice — who is connected to whom.

A conservative network drawn from convictions, civil verdicts, sentencing memos, indictments, and unsealed flight logs. Each line cites a specific court document. Vote on whether you think the connection is real — your votes are recorded with timestamps on /vote.

defamation policy. living people are named only when a public document — a verdict, a settlement, a sentencing memo, or an unsealed exhibit — already names them. The category of the edge tells you which: proven = court ruling, indicted = sworn charge not yet adjudicated, mentioned = appears in unsealed flight log or deposition without any charge attached. godding adds nothing to the public record; the swarm only updates this graph when something new is filed.

how to read this

  1. solid red lines are connections established by a court verdict — criminal conviction, civil liability, or settled with admission.
  2. dashed gold lines are connections from an active sworn indictment that has not yet reached verdict. presumed innocent until then.
  3. dotted purple lines are factual appearances in unsealed exhibits (e.g. flight logs, depositions). a name appearing in a list is not an accusation.
  4. godding does not add to any of these lists. the data only changes when a court does.

what this is not

this is not a conspiracy map. it is the opposite — a deliberately small graph that grows only when something is filed in court. if a name is missing, it's because nothing in the public record yet links them. that absence is a feature, not an oversight.
