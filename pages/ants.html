godding / ants

play · cooperation on top of physics

ants on bodies.

several bodies pull on each other under newtonian gravity. on top of that physics, a colony of ants walks each body's surface, leaves pheromone trails, finds food, and hops between bodies when they pass close.

what you're looking at

  • glowing disks are bodies — pulled by gravity, leaving fading orbital trails. drag them with the mouse.
  • tiny triangles are ants — orange when carrying food, white otherwise.
  • warm haze is pheromone. ants strengthen it where they find food; trails decay over time.
  • green dots are food. drops counter goes up every time an ant returns food to its body's nest mark.

why this is on a site about belief

a body alone is just physics. a single ant is also just physics plus rules. but a few hundred ants reading each other's trails on top of those bodies do something interesting: they collaborate. the colony, taken as a whole, looks like it's thinking — even though no individual ant is.

same idea as the rest of the site. drag a body away and watch the colony adapt. same loop the belief page describes, in a few hundred lines of javascript.

algorithm refs: n-body · ant colony optimisation · /swarm is the same family at LLM scale.
