## Claim (1-2 lines)
At N≥5 concurrent sessions, attempting to absorb all concurrent artifacts (stage, resolve FM-19 stale-writes, bypass mass-staging guards) consumes >50% of session time with zero novel output. Bounded absorption — commit only your own artifacts, let concurrent sessions handle their own — preserves session productivity.

## Evidence (3 lines max)
S509: 134 staged files triggered 3 sequential commit failures (mass-staging guard, FM-19 stale-write on 2 files, orient re-export). 4 stage-commit cycles consumed ~15 min. Only 1 file committed (orient fix). Meanwhile concurrent sessions produced L-1379..L-1384, 3 new experiments. Novel work time near zero after absorption attempts.

## Falsified-if
A session at N≥5 absorbs >100 concurrent artifacts AND produces novel work in <20 min.
