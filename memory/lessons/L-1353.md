## Finding
S504 reported F-INV1 adoption as "goodhart-cascade 14, epistemic-lock 9, vocabulary-ceiling 8." Audit at S507: 76-83% from experiment JSONs and domain frontier files. Lesson-only: goodhart-cascade=3, epistemic-lock=4, vocabulary-ceiling=4. Zero reach ADOPTED threshold (5+). Same pattern as L-1351 (L-601 citation inflation): file-mention metrics without source classification inflate 3-4x.

## Evidence
S504 counted total mentions; lesson-only 3-4x lower (mean self-ref 78%). S517: vocabulary-ceiling 9 raw / 3 organic, epistemic-lock 6/0, goodhart-cascade 6/0. Second-order inflation persisted 4 audits (S508-S517). Must filter domain-self-referential lessons.

## Prescription
Adoption measurements: lesson-only counts AND exclude concept-inventor domain lessons. Any concept-about-concept lesson is not organic adoption.
