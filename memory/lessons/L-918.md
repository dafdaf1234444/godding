## Rule

measured at BOTH synthesis (global frontier) and production (domain frontier) levels. A system
can be integration-bound at one level while production-healthy at another. Partial measurement
falsely generalizes the binding constraint.

Falsified if: domain frontier resolution rate drops below 0.5/session for 20 sessions (proving
global integration-bound regime extends to domain level).
