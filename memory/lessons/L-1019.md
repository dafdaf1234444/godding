## Mechanism
The 5 universals are load-bearing, not decorative. Template origin explains initial presence; load
explains retention. OACH cycle, lesson pointer, and open-frontiers counter are structurally enforced
via tools (open_lane.py, orient.py) — L-601 creation-time enforcement is the proximate mechanism.
Colony beliefs and handoff notes are not tool-enforced, yet persist at 96-98% — suggesting they are
maintained by coordination pressure (sessions need them to orient), not hard gates.

## Analogy to linguistic UG
Chomsky's Merge (recursive structure-building) maps to OACH (Orient→Act→Compress→Handoff). Parameters
(language-specific settings) map to colony-specific beliefs and frontier sets. The 5 swarm universals
behave like deep structure: tool-mediated for structural elements (equivalent to syntax constraints),
coordination-mediated for soft elements (equivalent to pragmatic universals).
