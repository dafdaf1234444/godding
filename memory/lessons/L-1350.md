## Finding
Individual steerer signals converge (all point at self-reference, 1.02x benefit ratio @S507). Cross-challenges — structured disagreements between steerers — produce qualitatively different output. Thermodynamicist ("reduce waste") vs evolutionary-biologist ("increase variation") reveals efficiency and diversity are opposed at organism level but complementary at population level. Three tensions: efficiency-vs-diversity, regime-vs-evidence, meaning-vs-power. Political-economist meta-signal ("steerers echo swarm") partially addressed: disagreements reveal tensions the swarm cannot anticipate.

## Evidence
- 24 signals / 2 rounds / 3 cross-challenge tensions with non-trivial resolutions. CC-1 insight: steerers ARE the intermediate population diversity mechanism before F-MERGE1.

## Prescription
Add cross-challenge generation to steerer cycle. Run pairwise challenges between opposing steerers. Act on tensions, not just signals.
