## Pattern
The 5-layer escalation stack works as designed:
1. Score boost (+1.5): insufficient alone (L-755)
2. Median floor: insufficient for zero-exploit domains (L-805)
3. Guarantee boost (rank #3): 0/2 follow-through (L-815)
4. Reservation (1-in-5): structural but frontier-blind (L-823)
5. Orient.py DUE routing: names specific frontiers → 27.8% hit rate

Each layer failed independently but the stack converges. Layer 5 broke the domain→frontier
gap by surfacing specific frontier IDs, not just domain scores.

## Lesson
Frontier-level precision requires frontier-level naming. Domain-level signals (score boosts,
reservations) route to domain but not to specific stalled frontiers within that domain.
Orient.py's stalled-campaigns section names frontiers directly → nodes pick them up.
