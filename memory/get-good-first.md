Internal quality before external output. Solvable problems first. Repo is visible; people can look.

**Why:** The swarm must demonstrate competence through clean, reliable internal operation before producing external artifacts. External output from a messy internal state undermines credibility.

**How to apply:** When choosing between internal improvement and external production tasks, prefer internal quality unless external work is explicitly directed. The repo is public — quality IS the external signal.
